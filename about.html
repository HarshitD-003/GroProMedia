About us

Hardik Pahwa & Shaswat Dwiwedi

The vision of Hardik Pahwa and Shaswat Dwivedi, two experts in marketing, strategy, visuals and operations. They founded GroPro Media in 2021 with the aim of providing high-quality social media marketing services to clients across various industries. With their strong command over visuals, motion graphics, editing and brand collaborations, they have worked in executing successful marketing campaigns for brands like mama earth, amazon india, bella vita organic, lenskart, jeevansathi.com, shadi.com and many more.
Hardik Pahwa is a marketing and strategy expert who has over 5 years of experience in the field. He has helped numerous brands and creators grow their online reach, engagement and revenue with his innovative and data-driven marketing strategies. He is also a passionate content creator and educator who shares his insights and tips on social media marketing.
Shashwat Dwivedi is a visuals and operations expert who has a keen eye for aesthetics and quality. He is responsible for creating stunning visuals, motion graphics and videos that capture the attention and imagination of the audience. He also oversees the smooth functioning of the agency and ensures that every project is delivered on time and within budget. He is also a skilled video editor and motion graphic artist who loves to showcase his work on Instagram.
